Solar Energy

Solar energy is the conversion of sunlight into usable energy forms. Solar photovoltaics (PV), solar thermal electricity and solar heating and cooling are well established solar technologies.

Solar PV power generation over a decade

Solar PV generation increased by a record 566 MW in 2019 to exceed 6000 MW. It demonstrated the third largest absolute generation growth of all renewable technologies in decade of 2011-2021, after wind. Solar PV is becoming the lowest-cost option for new electricity generation in most of the world, which is expected to propel investment in the coming years.
